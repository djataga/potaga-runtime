Conflicts & Escalation

Every material disagreement becomes a conflict card and climbs a bounded 4-level ladder — one receiver, one timeout, one decision rule per rung. Termination is guaranteed in at most 4 hops.

Escalation ladder

tie-break order: Security > Cost Ceiling > Deadline > Reversibility/Evidence > Reviewer Authority
L0 · 4 minLocal resolutionDisagreeing agents score the card independently. Accept only if top option beats runner-up by ≥ 15% and no hard constraint is violated.→
L1 · 2 minOrchestratorDeterministic tie-break rules. Security Override always prefers the more secure option, regardless of score margin.→
L2 · 5 minArchitectCross-domain conflicts decided on architectural fit and ADRs, with documented reasoning.→
L3 · unboundedHumanUser preferences, budget, or irreversible decisions. Orchestrator pauses and waits.
